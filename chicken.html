Chicken Tikka

For the Marination

  • 400g chicken breast cubes
  • 85g Greek yoghurt (you can use Dahi too)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli powder (I like it spicy)
  • 1 tbsp Garam masala
  • 1 tbsp Tandoori masala
  • 1 tsp Black salt
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp amchur powder (dried mango powder)
  • 1 tsp mustard oil
  • 1 tbsp Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1 tsp butter for cooking

Instructions

Add the chicken breast cubes to the marinade, ensuring that each piece is coated evenly. Cover the bowl and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 3-4 hours.
